# Weakeners Reference

These word categories dilute copy. Remove them on sight.

---

## Hedging Words
Make statements feel unsure or non-committal.

maybe, perhaps, possibly, probably, likely, unlikely, seem, seems, seemed, appears, apparently, suggests, can be seen as, could be, might be, may be

**Example:** "This will probably improve performance." → "This will improve performance."

---

## Softening Phrases
Used to avoid sounding confident. Usually unnecessary.

kind of, sort of, a little, a bit, somewhat, in a way, to some extent, more or less, almost, nearly

**Example:** "The UI is kind of confusing." → "The UI is confusing."

---

## Empty Intensifiers
Feel emphatic but add no meaning.

very, really, quite, extremely, highly, so, too, super, pretty (as adverb), totally, completely, absolutely, entirely, fully

---

## Filler Adverbs
Pad sentences without doing real work.

actually, basically, literally, clearly, obviously, essentially, generally, typically, usually, normally, virtually, effectively, largely, mostly

**Example:** "This basically means users sign in faster." → "This means users sign in faster."

---

## Authorial Throat-Clearing
Announces thinking instead of thinking.

I think, I believe, I feel, I would say, I would argue, we think, we believe, we feel, in my opinion, from my perspective, it seems to me

**Example:** "I think this is the best solution." → "This is the best solution."

---

## Passive-Aggressive Politeness
Weakens authority and clarity.

please note, just, simply, we just want to, you may want to, it would be great if, feel free to

**Example:** "You can just click the button below." → "Click the button below."

---

## Vague Quantifiers
Avoid commitment and precision.

some, several, many, few, various, a number of, a range of, a lot of, lots of, plenty of

---

## Redundant Framing Phrases
Delay the point instead of delivering it.

it is important to note that, it should be noted that, it is worth mentioning that, the fact that, there is / there are, it can be said that

**Example:** "It is important to note that users churn quickly." → "Users churn quickly."

---

## Over-Cautious Legalese
Sometimes necessary, often abused.

in most cases, under certain circumstances, as needed, where appropriate, if applicable, to the extent possible

---

## Weak Verb Phrases
Hide the action behind nouns.

| Weak | Strong |
|---|---|
| make a decision | decide |
| take action | act |
| give consideration to | consider |
| have an impact on | impact |
| play a role in | affect |
| provide an overview | overview |
| conduct an analysis | analyze |

---

## Talking About Talking
Stall momentum with meta-level commentary.

this article will, this section discusses, as mentioned earlier, as stated above, below we will see
